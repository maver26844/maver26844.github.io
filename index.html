Discover Mt. Hamrick

A very cozy town within the vast mountains.

About the our community

After Maverick Hamrick, a noble adventurer from York Tech, read the book "The House of the Spirits" by Isabelle Alende, he looked at the misdeeds that Esteban had done to the people of Tres Marias, and Mav strived to make something that surpassed Tres Marias to prove he could create something greater. He started by claiming an unnamed and unclaimed mountain as his own, calling it Mt. Hamrick, and recruiting all the people he knows and people they know to help him build a community.

One thing Maverick did not want was to be as controlling as Esteban was, so he let the people vote on what they would focus on. The one thing that Maverick used Tres Marias as inspiration for was the jobs. Because the population was so small, everyone was able to work on something, so everyone chose what they worked on in the community, whether it be gathering food, building houses, or being a teacher. Before citizens can work on anything that involves demolishing some form of nature or creating a structure of some kind, they must get permission from at least 1/3 of the community to do so.

Local School

Because the population is small, the local school of Herald Grade School is the only school, and covers every grade level. The school system itself focuses on getting its citizens prepared for a high ranking job so that they can achieve great things within this little mountain town.
